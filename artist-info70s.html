Annie Yi, also known as Annie Shizuka Inoh, is a Taiwanese singer, actress, and writer. She returned to Taiwan in 1988 to launch her singing career. In 2010, she was selected as a judge for the television series China's Got Talent.

Jacky Cheung Hok-yau is a Hong Kong singer, songwriter and actor. He is the best-selling Chinese music artist and one of the best-selling artists of all time, with sales of over 120 million records worldwide. Cheung has been dubbed the "God of Songs" of contemporary Chinese music and is one of the "Four Heavenly Kings".
